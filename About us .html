Our Journey

2018

Founding

Blue Ember Store was founded by a team of engineers and designers passionate about creating smarter home solutions. Our first prototype, the Blue Ember Smart Refrigerator, was developed in our San Francisco garage.

2020

Product Launch

We launched our first commercial products: the Blue Ember Smart Refrigerator and Blue Ember Energy-Smart Washer. Both received industry awards for innovation and design.

2021

Global Expansion

Blue Ember expanded to Canada and Europe, establishing our first international distribution centers and beginning partnerships with major retailers.

2022

Smart Ecosystem

We launched the Blue Ember Home App, creating a unified ecosystem that connects all our appliances for seamless control and intelligent automation.

Blue Ember became a certified carbon-neutral company, offsetting 100% of our operational emissions and implementing sustainable manufacturing practices across all facilities.
