At Evora Home Appliances, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If for any reason you're not happy with your product, we offer a straightforward refund and exchange process. This policy outlines the conditions, timelines, and procedures for returns, exchanges, and refunds on all Evora products.

1. Return Eligibility

Most new, unopened products purchased from Evora.com are eligible for return within the following timeframes:

Standard Returns

  • 30 days from delivery date: Full refund for new, unopened items in original packaging with all accessories, manuals, and tags.
  • Opened or used items: May be eligible for exchange or store credit at our discretion, subject to inspection.
  • Final sale items: Certain clearance, promotional, or customized items may be marked as "final sale" and are not eligible for return or exchange.

Defective or Damaged Products

If your Evora appliance arrives damaged or is defective:

  • Contact us within 7 days of delivery at support@evora.com
  • Provide photos of the damage and your order number
  • We will arrange a free replacement or full refund
  • Return shipping costs are covered by Evora for defective/damaged items

4. What We Cannot Accept

Unfortunately, we cannot accept returns for the following items:

  • Items returned after 30 days from delivery date
  • Products that have been installed, assembled, or modified
  • Items missing original packaging, accessories, or manuals
  • Products with signs of misuse, abuse, or improper maintenance
  • Consumable parts (filters, water lines, batteries, etc.) unless defective
  • Customized or personalized products (unless defective)
  • Clearance items marked as "Final Sale" at time of purchase
Note: Products returned in unsuitable condition may be refused or subject to a restocking fee of up to 15% of the purchase price.

5. Refunds & Exchanges

Refund Process

  • Refunds are issued to the original payment method used for purchase
  • Processing time: 5-10 business days after we receive and inspect your return
  • Original shipping charges are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error
  • Return shipping costs are free for defective or damaged items
  • For credit card payments, refunds may appear as a separate transaction

Exchange Process

  • Exchange for the same product: We'll ship your replacement immediately upon receiving your return
  • Exchange for a different product: We'll process your return and issue a refund, then you can place a new order
  • Price difference: If exchanging for a higher-priced item, you'll pay the difference. If exchanging for a lower-priced item, you'll receive a refund for the difference
  • Exchanges are subject to product availability

6. Return Shipping

We provide free return shipping for eligible returns:

  • Free return shipping: For defective, damaged, or wrong items shipped by Evora
  • Customer-paid shipping: For change-of-mind returns (except where required by law)
  • International returns: Customers are responsible for return shipping costs and any customs fees

When returning an item, please use a trackable shipping service and retain your tracking number. We're not responsible for lost return shipments.
